# Digital Wanderlust

## Style Prompt
"Digital Wanderlust" — A warm-tech aesthetic blending AI precision with travel wonder. Deep oceanic navy backgrounds with sunset amber accents, evoking intelligent adventure discovery. The palette balances technological sophistication (deep blue) with the warmth of travel (amber, teal).

## Colors
- Background: #0a1628 (deep ocean navy)
- Primary Text: #F8FAFC (warm white)
- Accent: #F59E0B (sunset amber)
- Secondary Accent: #0D9488 (travel teal)
- Subdued: #475569 (slate gray for secondary text)
- Card BG: #111827 (slightly lighter navy)

## Typography
- Headlines: "PingFang SC", "Noto Sans SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif / 700-900 weight / 64-96px
- Body/Subtitle: "PingFang SC", "Noto Sans SC", sans-serif / 300-400 weight / 28-36px
- Feature labels: Same family, 600 weight, 24-28px

## Motion
- Entrances: power3.out, 0.4-0.6s, varied directions (up, right, scale)
- Stagger: 0.1-0.15s between sibling elements
- Transitions: Push slide, 0.5s, power3.inOut

## What NOT to Do
- No pure black (#000) or pure white (#fff)
- No neon/cyan gradients (too cold for travel)
- No centered single-text-block layouts
- No gradient text effects
- No left-edge accent stripes on cards

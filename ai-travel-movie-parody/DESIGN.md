# Cinematic Parody — Digital Wanderlust

## Style Prompt
"Digital Wanderlust × Cinematic Homage" — A tech-animation aesthetic that pays homage to classic Chinese cinema through dramatic lighting, split-screen compositions, and bold typographic entrances. Deep navy backgrounds with sunset amber accents blend AI precision with the warmth of movie nostalgia. Each scene channels the visual language of an iconic film: 《大话西游》's mystical glow, 《功夫》's bold impact frames, 《无间道》's rooftop chiaroscuro, 《让子弹飞》's explosive reveals.

## Colors
- Background: #0a1628 (deep ocean navy)
- Primary Text: #F8FAFC (warm white)
- Accent: #F59E0B (sunset amber — hero moments, CTA)
- Secondary: #0D9488 (travel teal — feature cards)
- Subdued: #64748b (slate — supporting text)
- Card BG: rgba(17,24,39,0.85) (glass-like dark cards)
- Film Bar: #000000 (letterbox bars)
- Spotlight: rgba(245,158,11,0.12) (dramatic glow)

## Typography
- Font: "Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", sans-serif
- Hero Text: 900 weight, 96-128px, tight letter-spacing (0.02em)
- Subtitle: 400 weight, 28-36px, wide letter-spacing (0.12em)
- Movie Quote Style: 700 weight, 48-56px, centered
- Feature Labels: 600 weight, 26-30px
- CTA: 700 weight, 36-48px

## Motion
- Entrances: power3.out, 0.4-0.6s
- Stagger: 0.1-0.15s
- Transitions: Crossfade + scale push, 0.6s
- Dramatic beats: back.out easing for impact moments
- Ambient: slow glow breathing (sine.inOut, 3-4s cycles)

## What NOT to Do
- No pure black (#000) text on dark backgrounds
- No neon/cyan gradients (too cold)
- No centered single-text-block layouts without supporting elements
- No gradient text effects
- No unmotivated motion (everything enters/exits with purpose)
- No flat, evenly-lit scenes — always have lighting depth
